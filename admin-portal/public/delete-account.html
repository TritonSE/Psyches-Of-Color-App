Delete your account

In order to delete your account in the Psyches of Color mobile app, you have two options:

  1. Log into the mobile application, go to Profile -> Settings -> Delete my account. This will immediately delete your account and all associated data (username, password, onboarding responses, etc.)
  2. Email us at info@psychesofcolor.org asking us to delete your account. We will then manually delete your account and all associated data.
